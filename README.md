# Clockwise-Square

Input a number and out put a sqaure of number

Examples:

       21 22 23 22 25
       20 07 08 09 10
5 -->  19 06 01 02 11
       18 05 04 03 12
       17 16 15 14 13
       
       21 22 23 22 25 26
       20 07 08 09 10 27
6 -->  19 06 01 02 11 28
       18 05 04 03 12 29
       17 16 15 14 13 30
       36 35 34 33 32 31
       
       43 44 45 46 47 48 49
       42 21 22 23 22 25 26
       41 20 07 08 09 10 27
7 -->  40 19 06 01 02 11 28
       39 18 05 04 03 12 29
       38 17 16 15 14 13 30
